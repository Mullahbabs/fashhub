Beauty Tips & Tricks From Select Industry Professionals

Outfit & Style

Understanding your body shape is the first step to dressing beautifully. Use tailored pieces to highlight your assets and minimize areas you want to downplay.
Choose colors that complement your skin tone and use contrasting accessories to add a pop of personality.


Favor Ocheja
House Of Fierce

Accessories

Less is More, When wearing statement pieces, let them shine by keeping the rest of your outfit simple. A bold necklace or oversized earrings can elevate any look.
Don’t shy away from blending gold, silver, and rose gold. Modern accessories often work better when you combine textures.


Angela Fairy
Gewelry Ginie

Facebeat

Flawless Base Starts with Skin Prep. Always cleanse, exfoliate, and moisturize before applying makeup. Use a primer to create a smooth canva. The secret to perfect makeup lies in blending. Foundation, eyeshadow, or contour, seamless transitions make all the difference.


Marissa Ewa
Rissah MUA

Scent

Layer Scents for Uniqueness. Combine complementary perfumes and body lotions for a personalized, long-lasting aroma.
Apply to Pulse Points Focus on areas like your wrists, behind the ears, and the nape of your neck for a more noticeable scent throughout the day.


Daniel Obi
Odowgu Perfumery

Aura & Presence

Embrace Color Therapy" Wear colors that resonate with your energy for balance and positivity. Soft greens and blues calm the mind, while reds boost confidence.
Manifest Confidence. Practicing daily affirmations can help align your aura with your goals and exude positivity.


Kay_Ray
Mind Masters
